Sugar cookies

Description

This sugar cookie recipe is quick and easy to make with simple ingredients. They are delicious as-is or with candies mixed in. My friend uses chocolate mints on top, and they taste great!

Ingridients

  • Flour: This easy sugar cookie recipe starts with all-purpose flour.
  • Leaveners: Baking soda and baking powder act as leaveners, which means they help the cookies rise
  • Two sticks of butter add moisture and richness.
  • Of course, you'll need sugar for sugar cookies!
  • An egg lends more moisture and helps bind the cookie dough together.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)..
  2. Stir flour, baking soda, and baking powder together in a small bowl.
  3. Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth.
  4. Beat in egg and vanilla.
  5. Gradually blend in flour mixture.
  6. Roll dough into walnut-sized balls and place 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are golden, 8 to 10 minutes. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
